Bhatara Guru | Full Power

Bhatara Guru, the highest deity in Indonesian mythology, wielding unimaginable cosmic power with his divine weapons. As the embodiment of the universe's balance, Bhatara Guru holds dominion over the realms of time, space, and existence itself.

Character Stats

  • Tier: Boundless
  • Dimensionality: Omnipresent
  • Classification: Deity, Supreme Being
  • Age: Timeless
  • Gender: Male (Divine Form)

Powers & Abilities

  • Cosmic Manipulation: Bhatara Guru can manipulate the very fabric of the universe, controlling time, space, and dimensions with ease.
  • Reality Warping: Capable of altering the laws of reality, creating and destroying entire realms and realities at will.
  • Immortality: As an eternal being, Bhatara Guru cannot be killed, aging or weakened.
  • Omniscience: Possesses knowledge of all things across time, space, and beyond, allowing him to understand and anticipate all events.
  • Energy Projection: Bhatara Guru can project vast amounts of energy, capable of annihilating entire galaxies.
  • Telekinesis: He can move and manipulate objects across vast distances with his mind, including entire planets or realms.

Statistics

  • Strength: Boundless (Able to move and destroy entire realms)
  • Speed: Infinite (Able to move faster than time and space itself)
  • Durability: Omnipresent (Cannot be harmed by any conventional means)
  • Intelligence: Omniscient (Knows all that happens and will happen)
  • Agility: Divine (Can react and move across dimensions in an instant)
  • Stamina: Infinite (Eternal and tireless)
  • Range: Omnipresent (Can affect any point in the universe)

Hax Abilities

  • Reality Bending: He can change the laws of existence, creating and erasing anything at will.
  • Time Manipulation: Can freeze, accelerate, or reverse time across entire universes.
  • Dimensional Travel: Bhatara Guru can instantly travel across any and all dimensions, including those outside of existence.
  • Existential Erasure: Has the ability to erase any being or entity from existence, completely and permanently.
  • Creation and Destruction: He can create life or obliterate it with a single thought.

Equipment

Bhatara Guru wields several powerful divine weapons:

  • Senjata Ilahi: A sacred weapon with the power to bend reality itself.
  • Pralaya: A weapon of cosmic destruction, capable of ending entire universes.
  • Nirguna Brahman: A divine artifact that grants Bhatara Guru omnipotence and control over all things.
